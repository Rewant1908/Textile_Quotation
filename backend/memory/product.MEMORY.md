## Cotton Plain
- **sell_through_rate**: 70–85% (90 days benchmark)
- **avg_margin_per_meter**: ₹12–18
- **peak_season**: April–June (summer), October–November (Diwali)
- **dead_stock_threshold_days**: 45 (more lenient — staple category)
- **fastest_moving_designs**: White, Off-white, Light Grey, Pastel solid
- **slowest_moving_designs**: Dark solids in summer, Heavy weight in summer
- **recommended_floor_price**: cost + 15%
- **recommended_target_price**: cost + 25%
- **typical_price_range**: ₹45–90 per meter
- **gsm_range**: 100–180 GSM
- **business_notes**: Highest volume category. Year-round demand. Retailers stock this as baseline. Never let this go dead — reduce price before it reaches dead threshold.

## Silk

- **avg_margin_per_meter**: ₹40–80
- **peak_season**: October–December (Diwali + Wedding season), April–May (summer weddings)
- **dead_stock_threshold_days**: 60 (premium category — longer patience)
- **fastest_moving_designs**: Banarasi prints, Kanjivaram patterns, Solid jewel tones

- **gsm_range**: 80–200 GSM
